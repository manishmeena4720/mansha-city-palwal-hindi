LocationBackground

The beautiful City Palwal is situated on the national highway 2 from Delhi to Agra-Allahabad and falls in the national capital region just 40 km from south Delhi.It has incident touch from the time of Lord Krishna. It was prominent during the regime ofBritish as mahatma Gandhi was arrested for the first time from Railway station Palwal. It was initially a part of Gurgaon (Now Gurugram) District and then Faridabad District.
